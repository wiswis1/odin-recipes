Jollof Rice

This is a foolproof recipe for cooking Jollof rice, West Africa’s most popular dish. I like to serve it as a main with stewed chicken and fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • Rice - preferably long-grain parboiled rice, but any rice will do.
  • The main ingredient in Jollof rice is, of course, rice. I used long-grain parboiled rice for this recipe.
  • Next up are the1 canned tomato,2 fresh tomatoes,2 bell peppers ,1 scotch bonnet add more if you want more spice , onions,garlic , and ginger—
  • Oil, I use vegetable oil for my recipe.
  • Stock - Chicken, beef or vegetable stock are great choices to add more flavor to the dish.
  • Seasoning and herbs, - curry powder, salt, black/white pepper, boullion powder, thyme and bay leaves.
